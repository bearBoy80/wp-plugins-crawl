A tooltip, visible on hover, that says &#8220;opens a new window.&#8221;<br \/>\n3. &#8220;Opens a new window&#8221; screen reader text that will be read out to blind or visually impaired people using screen readers immediately following the link anchor text.<\/p>\n<p>Please note: new window warnings will be added to links and links that have been styled to look like buttons, including social media sharing and link icons.
