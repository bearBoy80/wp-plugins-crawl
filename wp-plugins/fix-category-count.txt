I used the following SQL to fix the problem and it ran very quickly.\n<blockquote>9300 rows affected. (Query took 2.9573 seconds.)<\/blockquote>\n\n<pre><code>UPDATE wp_term_taxonomy SET count = (\nSELECT COUNT(*) FROM wp_term_relationships rel \n    LEFT JOIN wp_posts po ON (po.ID = rel.object_id) \n    WHERE \n        rel.term_taxonomy_id = wp_term_taxonomy.term_taxonomy_id \n        AND \n        wp_term_taxonomy.taxonomy NOT IN (&#039;link_category&#039;)\n        AND \n        po.post_status IN (&#039;publish&#039;, &#039;future&#039;)\n)\n<\/code><\/pre>\n\nSource:
