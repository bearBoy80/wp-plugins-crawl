I chose to integrate Teachable with WooCommerce because I wanted to have a separate platform for hosting my videos \/ courses (like Teachable), yet have the sales come through in one place (WooCommerce). I have a variety of different products sold on my site and thus want to be able to upsell or cross-sell courses with those other products. This plugin allows me to create products within WooCommerce and automatically enrol students to a course on Teachable once they've purchased that product. How awesome is that?? This way customers won't need to create an account on my main website so the student enrolment is completely outsourced to Teachable. With plugins like MemberPress that turn your Wordpress website into a course hosting site you have to \"force\" every customer, whether interested in your course or not to create an account, which can drive down sales and just isn't fun :D For this plugin to work properly you do need to have the Teachable API function enabled which you will have access to within the free trial or their higher tier plan, so it's a bit pricy.
