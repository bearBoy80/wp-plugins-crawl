It aims to standardize data protection laws across all EU member states, safeguarding EU citizens&#8217; personal data while regulating how data is processed by organizations. The GDPR enhances individuals&#8217; control over their personal data, granting rights like access to data, correction, deletion, and the option to object to data processing under certain circumstances.
