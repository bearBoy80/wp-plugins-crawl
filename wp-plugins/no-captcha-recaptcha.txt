The link should be moved to the Settings menu, as a submenu item.\n\nScreenshot of #1: https:\/\/s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com\/elasticbeanstalk-us-west-2-868470985522\/ShareX\/2017\/04\/chrome_2017-04-29_14-14-25.png\n\nIssue #2) The ReCaptcha widget is clearly lacking some bottom margin on the login screen. The error should say something like:\n\n\"ERROR: Please retry ReCAPTCHA\" - at the very least, refer to the brand as this is NOT a regular captcha field.\n\n\"ERROR: Please confirm you are not a robot\"\n\n\"ERROR: Please check the box below to prove you are a human\"\n\netc.\n\nEverything else looks good.
